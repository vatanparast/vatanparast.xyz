For Iranian Women - برای زنان ایران

December 25, 2022 Iran 94 words 1 min

Recent incidents about the deaths of people in Iran are disturbing. Killing, arresting, and threatening people is not how to treat Iran’s brave women and men. What a shame to arrest Taraneh Alidoosti, a citizen, mom of a nine-year-old daughter, and award-winning actress. It is cowardly to shut internet access and block common communication apps aiming to isolate people from each other and the rest of the world. I am deeply upset about the current situation and stand with the people.
