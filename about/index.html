About

May 14, 2022 81 words 1 min

I was born and raised in Iran. I have a master’s degree in Plant Biology from Tarbiat Modares University in Tehran, Iran, and a PhD in Plant Evolutionary Biology from Chiba University in Japan. Since then, I have done postdocs in Japan, USA, Denmark, and UK.

I have molecular laboratory skills and gained knowledge of genomic data analyses. Besides science, I am interested in art, literature, and new technologies. This website is created using the Hugo framework and hosted on GitHub.
